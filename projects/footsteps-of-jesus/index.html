In the Footsteps of Jesus

For this project I worked closely with the author to source and select over 200 images depicting historical locations referenced in the Bible. I located photographers who had traveled to out of the way locations and obtained permission to use their images in the book.

Working on a very tight budget we were able to create a beautiful book, which presents a never before seen journey through the Holy Land.
