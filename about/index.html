About Me

Hi, I'm Rob, a freelance photo-editor and front-end web developer from Alexandria, VA. For the last 10 years I've been working in the publishing industry as photo-researcher & editor, striving to find the perfect visual accompaniment for whatever other content there might be. I take the same approach to front-end deveolpment, helping people pair their content with the right system and design. I've worked on a wide range of projects across a variety of topics; everything from mueseum exhibits highlighting corporate histories to books on mythological creatures. I enjoy the challenges each new project brings and love figuring out the best ways to deal with them. I'd love to work with you.
